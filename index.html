What is TikFlow Scheduler?

TikFlow Scheduler is a lightweight web application designed to help content creators and marketing teams manage their TikTok publishing workflow.

📁

Upload Video Content

Easily upload your video files and manage metadata in one place.

📅

Schedule in Advance

Plan your content calendar and set the perfect time for your posts.

🚀

Official API Publishing

Publish videos directly to TikTok via approved Content Posting APIs.

📊

Simple Dashboard

Manage all your scheduled and published content in a clean interface.

How it works

Our integration follows TikTok's official developer guidelines to ensure a secure and seamless experience.

01

Connect your TikTok account

Users securely log in using TikTok Login Kit and authorize access to the necessary scopes.

02

Upload your content

Users upload video files and add captions, hashtags, and privacy settings within the app interface.

03

Schedule or publish

Users can either publish immediately or schedule posts using TikTok Content Posting API.

04

Content goes live

Videos are published directly to the user’s TikTok account through official integration without manual intervention.

Data Usage & Privacy

TikFlow Scheduler only accesses TikTok data that users explicitly authorize. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

We use data to:

  • Enable login via TikTok
  • Upload and publish user content
  • Provide scheduling functionality

We do NOT:

  • Sell user data to third parties
  • Share data with unauthorized parties
  • Store unnecessary personal information

All data usage complies with TikTok Developer Terms and applicable privacy regulations.
